Range of services

I know that buying your own apartment is not buying socks, but the most important decision in your life, but once you decide on it, it's time to collect it from the developer.

This is an extremely important moment for the new owner because many developers apply the rule that if no defect was indicated on the review of the apartment, then there is no such defect at all. You also hear a lot about the different quality of work and defects even in new flats, these is why it is so important to check the whole apartment in detail.

It is at this crucial moment that I would like to help you. I offer a full technical and visual inspection of the apartment.

The scope of the offer includes checking the entire apartment and parking spaces / storage rooms belonging to this apartment.

In the apartment, I will check all its elements, including:

  • plasters
  • screeds
  • windows, especially glass
  • entrance door
  • radiators
  • window sills
  • balcony: tiles, elevation, balustrades
  • installations according to the project

On request, I will also check the area of the apartment.

If you have any questions, I will give you exhaustive answers.

All checks are made with professional equipment based on applicable standards.

I would like to prepare myself before the investigation, so please send me an e-mail with the apartment design and the prospectus informing about the standard of finish.

Ask the developer to wash the windows before collection so that I can spot any defects.

Your satisfaction is my pleasure...

I encourage you to contact me!
